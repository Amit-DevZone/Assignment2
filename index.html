Chocolate Cake

Preparation Time: 30 minutes

Serving Size: 8 servings

  • 200 grams golden caster sugar
  • 200 grams unsalted butter (softened, plus extra for the tins)
  • 4 large eggs
  • 200 grams self-raising flour
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 4 tablespoons milk
Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
Mix sugar and unsalted butter until smooth.
Add eggs one at a time, beating well.
In another bowl, mix self-raising flour and cocoa powder.
Then add the vanilla extract.
Combine all ingredients and pour into a baking pan.
Bake for 30 minutes.
